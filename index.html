ANCHOR FOUNDATION





    Anchor Foundation was born from a passion and desire to be a positive force in changing the future lives of South Africa.
    From humble beginnings, The Foundation went on to grow exponentially thereby creating a positive impact in communities.
    With underprivilegded communities spanning the entire country, We are to make a sustainable difference in the lives of those we counter, Their families and their communities.
    Our communities are some of the least resourced and marginalised in the country, But as long as there is a passion a commitment to empower, Together with our partners, We are able tobridge many of these gaps.

Mission


    Our mission is to reach the unreached through community based project, Empowering them through all aspects of life to create healthy and strong individuals

Vision


    Our vision is to create vibrant, stable communities where each person is afforded the oppurtinities to improve their lives to the maximum potential, contributing postively towards their families and to generations to come.

Strategic Objectives


    Ensure delivery of quality and best value programs within out communities.
    Ensure effective programme implementation to support the improvement of Social Development learning
    Establish and maintain a reputable, capable, innovative dynamic organization.


Anchor Foundation: Social Development


    Through a holistic, Inclusive model called "Know Thy Self", We aim to empower individuals by bringing out their hidden talents and gifts, Giving them the tools to be creative while enabling them to sustain their livelihood.
    Encourage leadership by teaching life skills and learning.
    By using a holistic approach, We are able to address and overcome most obstacles that stand in the way of providing quality lifestyle.
